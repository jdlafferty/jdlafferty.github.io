Senior Editor Instructions

The objective of the JDS two-tier reviewing system is to create a high impact, high quality journal, while helping to make the reviewing process more efficient. Senior Editors make decisions on whether submissions will be sent for full review; these decisions play an important role in shaping the scope of the journal. Senior Editors are asked to make a judgment about whether or not a paper could make an interesting, high quality and potentially impactful contribution to the broad data science community.

The following criteria are used:

01. Is the work of high quality ?

02. Are the main results clear and interesting ?

03. Are the contributions of the paper significantly new, and not incremental ?

04. Does the paper solve an important problem, or open up a new line of research ?

This first stage of reviewing is completed within three weeks of submission, and authors are promptly notified of the decision.
